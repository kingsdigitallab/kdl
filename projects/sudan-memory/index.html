Sudan Memory

Sudan Memory aims to protect through conservation and digitisation a range of heritage materials that reside in the National Record Office of Sudan and the content holders of the National Team for the Digitisation of Cultural Heritage consortium. This work includes building capacity by teaching skills such as collection and conservation assessment; cataloguing; digitisation; digital preservation; digital project development; and project management. KDL supported the PI in an advisory role, providing technical expert knowledge and analysis.
