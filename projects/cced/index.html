CCED: Clergy of the Church of England Database Project CCED

The Clergy of the Church of England Database was established in October 1999; its objective to construct a relational database containing the careers of all clergymen of the Church of England between 1540 and 1835. The Database fills a major gap in our knowledge of one of the most important professions in early modern England and Wales.
