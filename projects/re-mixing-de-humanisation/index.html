(Re) Mixing (de) Humanisation: Virtual Reality Migration Stories for Hard to Reach Audiences with Museums of Migration RTB

The project seeks to explore the potential for communicating exhibits, stories and personal experiences using immersive technology. It will focus on transmitting, replicating, or otherwise porting, the Room to Breathe temporary exhibition showing at the UK Migration Museum. Potential audiences include children in group participation activities at their schools, lay-persons wishing to experience an exhibition remotely from their homes, and partner museums who might wish to share ideas, exhibits and artefacts in real-time, or recreate museum experiences at a distance.
