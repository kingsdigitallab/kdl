What if my project is already funded when I contact KDL?

We will consider your project as described in How does KDL decide whether to take on a project?. If we take it on, we will follow a process similar in parts to our Feasibility Assessment but quicker. The output in this case will be a Statement of Work with a MoSCoW-prioritized list of requirements tailored to the available budget. And like our Product Quote, the Statement of Work is a formal agreement which will be signed by you and KDL.
