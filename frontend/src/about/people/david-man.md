David has extensive experience designing digital products across a broad range of sectors including e-learning, children's education, mobile, publishing and broadcast media.

He  worked at the BBC for 15 years where he designed the BBC Sport London 2012 Olympic website and BBC News's first native mobile apps. In BBC Research & Development he worked on projects which focused on  content discovery, transparency and agency in technology and digital public service values.

David is continually excited by the challenge of using design and technology to solve complex problems.
